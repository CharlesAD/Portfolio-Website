About

I think the thing that enticed me most about technology is how it has provided me an outlet to express myself creatively by allowing me to take my ideas and turn them into something tangible. Technology is what makes the world go around and it’s improving the lives of vast amounts of people. I receive great satisfaction from problem-solving and harnessing my creativity. This enthusiasm for technology has consistently fuelled my dedication to continuous learning in the field.

Experience

I began my venture into the world of freelance development with a proactive approach – I started by creating websites for free to cultivate my skills. As I progressed from creating free website projects to working with paying clients, I realised the immense value of hands-on experience in refining my skills and navigating client interactions. Adding to this journey, my commitment to coding excellence was recognized through my selection as one of only ten participants in the Rubik Talents coding skills Boot-Camp. This accomplishment, being one of 12 after surpassing hundreds of applicants in a rigorous selection process, speaks volumes about my distinct capabilities and determination to stand out.
